Projects

ProletariRat

A game about a humble rat magically endowed with superpowers trying to survive in the heart of Paris by defeating rude customers in a famous restaurant. Story and setting heavily inspired by the 2007 film Ratatouille and loosely relating to concepts of class disadvantages. The game is played using the HTML canvas, created almost purely in JavaScript, and designed with basic game development concepts in mind. Don't forget to unmute the tab to hear the lovely music and I hope you enjoy! (Hint: If you die a lot, press Spacebar to skip the intro.)

Wordle Solver

Most people probably know the famous New York Times game titled "Wordle". Every day, it gives the user 6 tries to guess the word by putting in guesses and seeing which letters were wrong, which partly right, or which in the correct spot. Well, no longer do people need to think of the words themselves since the script I have written in python does everything for them, down to the opening of the window and the typing of the guesses. It uses a very primitive way of searching, I will admit, but it works!
